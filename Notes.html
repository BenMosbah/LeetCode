World Ladder II

I tried a DFS that got all possible paths and then chose the shortest one. Did not pass the time limit test. Next steps will be to try BFS which I think is more suited. Second time, I tried DFS. This time it relies on a while loop and at each iteration of the while loop, we only test chains that have the same length. This avoids looking for longer paths once paths of length k have been found. Unfortunately, even this was not fast enough. My third idea is to try a DFS again but from both ends : from the left, extending beginWord and from the right extending endWord. And at each time testing if we can "stick" chains together.
